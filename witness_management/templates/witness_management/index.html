Welcome to OHS Express

Mission

Dedicated to the protection of the environment, natural communities and the public, we are guided by our relentless focus to constantly strive to implement critical initiatives required to achieve our vision. In doing this, we will deliver operational excellence to our customers and provide a method for them to meet or exceed their commitments relating to the protection of the environment, public safety, legislative requirements and their fiscal responsibilities to their stakeholders.

Vision

We will participate in areas where technology can support the protection of the environment and public safety in partnership with the pipeline transportation industry. We will make significant contributions through innovation and business process effectiveness by developing technology that fosters lean process execution and process control through the use of state of the art technology at a reasonable price to assist our customers in engaging in sustainable and responsible practices

Benefits

Transparency of workflow and information between departments

Consistency of flow of information between departments

Data is available in realtime

Expanded analysis and reporting capabilities through process integration and a single view of all data supporting the integrity management program
